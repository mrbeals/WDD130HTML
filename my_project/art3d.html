Joceline's 3D Art Journey

After I finished high school I wanted to continue to grow my art skills. I researched different mediums saw a few videos on different artists. 3D art caught my attention. After working on 2D art work for so long it was such a new refreshing change. I always loved creating art and bringing it to life but 3D was exciting. It takes bringing art to life in an entire new level. I love getting my hands dirty and making 3D art. This is most rewarding part of creating in this form. It reminds me of when I was younger and would scrap many pieces of paper along with random materials to make art in kindergarten. I feel like that little girl again, she still lives within myself.
