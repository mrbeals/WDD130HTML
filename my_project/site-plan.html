Content

Home page

Welcome to Joceline's corner, where Creativity and meaning meet to form wonderous peaces of art. Dive into the world and mind of Joceline, as she conveys her meaning with you through different mediums of art, including Acrylic, 3D, and Digital art. Award winning for her pieces, Joceline seeks to share these messages with you. Whether you are new to art, or have enjoyed art for a long time. We are confident that you will find a piece among this collection that speaks directly to you.

Joceline Began art in elementary exploring with anything she could get her hands on. Then moving to painting when she was first gifted paints. She would scribble alot. She would create for her enjoyment and fun but as she got older art began meaning so much more to her. It was a way for her to express herself and through the moods and creations she would capture through practice and a steady hand. She persued art in highschool, winning awards in the school contest. She then moved to pursue it as a career, wanting to bring joy and meaning into the lives of those who saw it. As well as a way to worship God. She now pursues Art as a College student at BYU-Idaho, studying to receive her illustration degree.

3D Art

Joceline made this piece by using everyday cardboard and paper scraps. She sculpted this piece using mainly boxes that would have otherwise been recycled or thrown away. It is exciting to see things 3 dimensional, and it helps Joceline to see new potential. She enjoys trying new things. When she sees something 3D it makes it feel real to her, and she wants to convey this meaning to others.

Peaceful House - I wanted to portray the emotion of joy what it feels and looks life. The brilliance and bright colors. Along with the warmth of that feeling. The home has always been an inspiration of mine. What it looks like feels like, what it can be. Nature will always play a big part and continue to inspire me in many works of art. God reminds me that family is grounded like roots of a tree. This piece was created out of cardboard materials, water color, and acrylic paint. Each layer was carefully placed to create this 3D model.

Arylic Art

My Potential - I wanted to show a reflection of who we each are and how we can accomplish great things. I have always found swans beautiful, holding great meaning. To relate and reflect on how we should see ourselves. In a kinder way how God sees us, and the potential we have.

Happiness - I wanted to portray the emotion of joy, what it feels and looks like. The brilliance and bright colors. Along with the warmth of the feeling.

Clear Mind - Sometimes we can feel stuck, no matter what it is. Our emotinos and feelings are part of who we are. Nature reminds me of different emotions within myself. Dark clouds remind me of what is around the corner. It also reminds me of peace. Dark clouds are beautiful but as temporary as well. I can clear my mind by remembering this always.
